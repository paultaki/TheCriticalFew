Focus on the Critical Few

Summary

Embrace the “80/20 rule” to focus on the critical few, not the trivial many:

  • Delegate to unlock your team’s potential—and your own time.
  • Keep communication and directives simple, cutting out unnecessary filler.
  • Trust your frontline employees; they have valuable insights senior leaders often miss.
  • “Carrying the bag” (doing the frontline job) builds credibility with your team.
  • Encourage collaboration by ensuring every voice is heard—especially the quiet ones.
  • Celebrate strategic risk-taking and learn from failures.
  • Protect your nights and weekends by honing in on what truly matters.
  • Believe in yourself and your people; a cohesive, empowered team can break records.
